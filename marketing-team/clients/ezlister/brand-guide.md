# Brand Guide

## Mission

To give Etsy sellers their time back — by replacing scattered, tedious listing workflows with one fast, purpose-built toolkit.

## Vision

Become the essential operating system for Etsy sellers — the first tool they open when it's time to list, update, or grow their shop.

## Values

- **Efficiency first** — Every feature exists to save real time. If it doesn't make a seller's workflow faster, it doesn't ship.
- **Seller-built** — Designed around actual Etsy workflows, not adapted from generic tools. We know the pain points because we've lived them.
- **Simplicity** — Powerful tools with zero learning curve. Open it, use it, move on.
- **Reliability** — Your images, your listings, your data — always accessible, always working. No surprises.
- **Empowerment** — Help small sellers operate like big ones. Scale your shop without scaling your workload.

## Brand Story

Running an Etsy shop means wearing every hat — maker, photographer, editor, listing manager, marketer. The creative part is why sellers started. The busywork is what slows them down.

Cropping listing photos one by one. Switching between mockup tools and image editors. Scrolling through Etsy's dashboard to find a listing buried in hundreds of others. These small tasks add up to hours every week — hours that could be spent creating, promoting, or just living.

ezListr was built to fix that. Not as a bloated design suite or a generic business tool, but as a focused toolkit that does exactly what Etsy sellers need: crop photos to spec, build mockups, store images, and manage listings — all in one place. No extra features you'll never use. No learning curve. Just the tools that make listing day faster.

## Tagline

**The toolkit for serious Etsy sellers.**

Alternates:
- Less busywork. More sales.
- Your Etsy shop, streamlined.

## Elevator Pitch

ezListr is a web-based toolkit that helps Etsy sellers crop listing photos to spec, build product mockups, store images across tools, and manage listings in bulk — all from one place. Free to start, built for sellers who treat their shop like a business.
