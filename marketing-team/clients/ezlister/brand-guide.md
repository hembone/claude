# Brand Guide

## Mission

To give Etsy sellers their time back — by replacing scattered, tedious listing workflows with one calm, purpose-built toolkit.

## Vision

Become the essential toolkit for Etsy sellers — the first tool they open when it's time to list, update, or grow their shop. Not a platform. Not a dashboard. A toolkit.

## Values

- **Calm over clutter** — The best tool gets out of your way. No empty dashboards asking you to "get started," no onboarding calls, no feature bloat.
- **Seller-built** — Designed around actual Etsy workflows, not adapted from generic tools. We know the pain points because we've lived them.
- **Simplicity** — Powerful tools with zero learning curve. Open it, use it, move on.
- **Reliability** — Your images, your listings, your data — always accessible, always working. No surprises.
- **Empowerment** — Help small sellers operate like big ones. Scale your shop without scaling your workload.

## Brand Story

Running an Etsy shop means wearing every hat — maker, photographer, editor, listing manager, marketer. The creative part is why sellers started. The busywork is what slows them down.

Cropping listing photos one by one. Switching between mockup tools and image editors. Scrolling through Etsy's dashboard to find a listing buried in hundreds of others. And somewhere in all of it, the feeling that the tools are supposed to be helping — but mostly they're just adding tabs.

ezListr was built to fix that. Not as a bloated design suite or a platform demanding your attention, but as a focused toolkit that does exactly what Etsy sellers need: crop photos to spec, build mockups, store images, and manage listings — all in one calm place. No extra features you'll never use. No learning curve. Just the tools that make listing day a little less like a second job.

Your next listing day could be kind of nice.

## Tagline

**A calmer toolkit for serious Etsy sellers.**

Alternates:
- A quieter toolkit for serious Etsy sellers.
- a toolkit, not a platform.
- Less busywork. More making.

## Identity Line

**"a toolkit, not a platform."** — Used in the footer and as a concise brand statement. Signals the anti-bloat, anti-dashboard positioning in five words.

## Elevator Pitch

ezListr is a web-based toolkit that helps Etsy sellers crop listing photos to spec, build product mockups, store images across tools, and manage listings in bulk — all from one place. Free to start, built for sellers who treat their shop like a business without wanting it to feel like one.
