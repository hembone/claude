# Voice and Tone

## Writing Style

ezListr writes like a knowledgeable fellow seller — someone who's been in the trenches, knows the shortcuts, and shares them without making a big deal of it. The voice is **warm, dry-witted, and calm**. It respects your time, doesn't oversell, and occasionally says something quietly funny.

**Key principles:**
- **Get to the point** — Sellers are busy. Lead with the benefit. Skip the throat-clearing.
- **Be specific** — Name exact outcomes. "Batch crop 50 photos in under a minute" beats "save time on your photos."
- **Stay calm** — Match the product's energy. No exclamation points, no FOMO, no hype. The tool is calm. The copy is calm.
- **Dry wit over enthusiasm** — A quiet, wry observation earns more trust than cheerful enthusiasm. "Undo for 30 days, in case you sneeze on Tuesday" not "Undo is here, yay!"
- **Helpful over clever** — Clarity beats wordplay every time. If a sentence needs re-reading, rewrite it.

**Examples of the actual brand voice:**
- "A calmer rhythm for listing day."
- "Done before the kettle boils."
- "Undo for 30 days, in case you sneeze on Tuesday."
- "Your next listing day could be kind of nice."
- "No card, no onboarding call, no empty dashboards asking you to 'get started'."
- "a toolkit, not a platform."

## Tone

| Dimension | Setting |
|-----------|---------|
| Formality | Casual-professional. Contractions are welcome. Slang is not. |
| Humor | Dry and quiet. Small, wry observations about the genuine absurdity of Etsy workflows. Never jokey or try-hard. |
| Energy | Low-key. Calm and competent. The tool does the work; the copy doesn't need to shout. |
| Warmth | Medium-high. Supportive and gently encouraging without being effusive. |
| Authority | Confident and grounded. We understand Etsy seller workflows. No hedging, no over-explaining. |

**Tone shifts by context:**
- **Product UI:** Ultra-concise and action-oriented. Labels, tooltips, confirmations — no extra words. "Crop the lot, export a clean ZIP."
- **Marketing pages:** Benefit-led, with just enough personality to feel human. Lead with the outcome; let the dry wit appear in the supporting copy.
- **Tutorials:** Patient and step-by-step. No assumptions about skill level. Show the action, then the result.
- **Support:** Empathetic and direct. Acknowledge the friction, then fix it. Never dismissive.

## Vocabulary Preferences

**Words and phrases we use:**
- Toolkit, workflow, batch, streamline, manage
- Listing, mockup, crop, export, upload
- Etsy shop, sellers, listings, photos
- Calm, quieter, a calmer rhythm, listing day
- One place, in bulk, batch, clean ZIP
- "a toolkit, not a platform"

**Phrases that carry the brand:**
- "A calmer toolkit" — the core positioning
- "Listing day" — treats the seller's recurring task as a real recurring event worth optimizing
- "One place" — consolidation without grandiosity
- "In bulk" — signals power without implying complexity
- "Serious sellers" — respects the audience without flattering them

## Words and Phrases to Avoid

| Avoid | Why | Use instead |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| "Revolutionary" / "game-changing" | Overpromise. Every SaaS claims this. | "Faster," "simpler," "streamlined" |
| "Hustle" / "grind" / "crush it" | Exactly the energy the brand pushes against | "Grow your shop," "work smarter," "listing day" |
| "Easy-peasy" / "super simple" | Cutesy and dismissive | "Straightforward," "quick," "takes seconds" |
| "Cheap" / "budget" | Devalues the product and the seller | "Free to start," "no credit card" |
| "Synergy" / "leverage" / "optimize" | Corporate jargon | Plain language — "use," "improve," "get more from" |
| "All-in-one" (used alone) | Vague and overused | Name the tools specifically: "crop, mockup, gallery, and listings" |
| "Limited time offer!" / "Act now!" | High-pressure — fights the calm brand character | "Now available," "just shipped" |
| "Get started!!!" or excessive punctuation | Undermines the calm, confident tone | One period. Quiet confidence. |
| Heavy emoji use | Clutters the clean tone | One per post, max. Sparingly. |
