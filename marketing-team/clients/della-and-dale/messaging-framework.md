## Differentiators

### vs. Mass-market wall art (Amazon, Target, IKEA)
**Della & Dale feels curated and personal.** Big-box wall art is designed to offend no one, which means it inspires no one. Our prints are chosen with a specific eye — vintage, warm, collected. You're buying taste, not just a product.

### vs. Other Etsy digital art shops
**Consistent aesthetic and curatorial identity.** Many Etsy shops sell hundreds of disconnected prints in every style. Della & Dale has a recognizable look. If it's in the shop, it fits the vision. Customers trust the taste.

### vs. Physical art and framed prints
**Fraction of the cost, instant delivery.** A single framed print from a home decor store runs $40–$150. Our entire catalog costs less than one of those frames. Plus, no shipping damage, no waiting.

### vs. Free art on Pinterest/Google
**Print-ready quality and thoughtful presentation.** Free downloads are often low-resolution, poorly formatted, or watermarked. Our files are high-resolution, properly sized for standard frames, and ready to print without any editing.

## Competitive Positioning

**Position:** The curated boutique in a sea of marketplaces.

Della & Dale sits at the intersection of **affordable** and **tasteful** — a space most competitors miss. Mass-market is affordable but generic. Boutique art shops are tasteful but expensive. We deliver both.

**Positioning statement:**
For home decor lovers who want their space to feel personal and collected, Della & Dale is the digital art shop that offers curated, vintage-inspired prints as instant downloads — because beautiful walls shouldn't require a big budget or a long wait.
