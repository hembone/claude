# Brand Guide

## Mission

To make beautiful, curated wall art accessible to everyone — bringing warmth, character, and intention into everyday spaces through affordable digital prints.

## Vision

Become the go-to destination for thoughtfully curated downloadable art that transforms any room into a space that feels collected, personal, and timeless.

## Values

- **Accessibility** — Beautiful art shouldn't require a gallery budget. We keep prices low so anyone can elevate their space.
- **Intentional curation** — Every piece is selected and presented with care. We don't do filler — if it's in the shop, it belongs there.
- **Warmth over perfection** — We favor art that feels lived-in, collected, and personal over anything sterile or mass-produced.
- **Heritage with a modern eye** — We draw from vintage and classical sources but present them in a clean, contemporary way.
- **Simplicity** — In our art, our brand, and our customer experience. No clutter, no friction.

## Brand Story

Della & Dale is named after the grandparents who shaped our sense of home — the kind of home where every object on the wall had a story, where beauty lived in the details, and where things were chosen with care, not just convenience.

That spirit carries into everything we do. We curate vintage botanical illustrations, classical art, and nature-inspired prints and make them available as instant digital downloads — so you can build a space that feels warm, collected, and unmistakably yours, without the designer price tag.

What started as a love for vintage flora has grown into a broader vision: a curated collection of downloadable wall art spanning botanicals, nature, classical illustration, and beyond. Every print is chosen to bring something quiet and beautiful into your everyday.

## Tagline

**Art that feels like home.**

Alternates:
- Curated prints. Instant warmth.
- Walls worth looking at.

## Elevator Pitch

Della & Dale is a digital art shop offering curated, affordable wall art as instant downloads. Rooted in vintage botanical illustration and expanding into broader art styles, we help home decor lovers, first-time decorators, and design-minded shoppers transform their spaces with prints that feel collected and personal — all for under $5.
