# Content Guidelines

## Approved Topics

- Wall art styling tips (gallery walls, frame selection, print sizing, room placement)
- Home decor inspiration and room tours
- Vintage botanical art history and illustration techniques
- Seasonal decorating ideas and color trends
- Behind-the-scenes of how prints are sourced and curated
- Interior design trends and recaps (aligned with warm/neutral aesthetic)
- "How to print digital downloads" tutorials and guides
- Gift guides featuring downloadable art
- Before/after room transformations using affordable art
- Plant and botanical appreciation (natural tie-in to the catalog)

## Content Pillars

### 1. Product Showcase
New prints, collection drops, styled mockups, close-up details. The core of the content calendar — showing the art in context.

### 2. Styling & Decor Tips
Gallery wall layouts, frame guides, "how to style this print," room-by-room inspiration. Positions Della & Dale as a taste authority, not just a shop.

### 3. Trends & Culture
Home decor trends, color of the year recaps, design movement explainers, seasonal shifts. Informational content that builds authority and drives discovery through search and shares.

### 4. Behind the Curation
How prints are selected, the stories behind vintage illustrations, what makes a good botanical print. Builds trust and differentiates from shops that feel anonymous.

## Platform-Specific Rules

### Social Media

#### Instagram
- **Format:** Mix of static posts (styled mockups), carousels (collections, tips), and Reels (room styling, process, trends)
- **Grid aesthetic:** Warm, cohesive, airy. Alternate between product shots and lifestyle/tip content. Never more than two product posts in a row.
- **Captions:** Short and evocative for product posts. Longer and informative for tips/trends. Always end with a soft CTA or question, never "LINK IN BIO!!!"
- **Stories:** Behind-the-scenes, polls ("which print for this room?"), new drop teasers, customer features
- **Hashtags:** 10–15 per post. Mix of niche (#digitalwallart #botanicalprint #gallerywall) and broader (#homedecor #apartmentdecor #etsyfinds). No spammy or irrelevant tags.
- **Posting cadence:** 3–4 feed posts/week, daily stories when active

#### Pinterest
- **Format:** Tall pins (2:3 ratio) with styled mockups. Clean, minimal text overlay if any.
- **Boards:** Organized by room (living room art, bedroom art), style (botanical, vintage, gallery wall), and seasonal collections
- **Descriptions:** Keyword-rich, natural language. Include "digital download," "printable wall art," "instant download," frame sizes.
- **Strategy:** Pinterest is the discovery engine. Optimize every pin for search. Link directly to Etsy listings.
- **Posting cadence:** 5–10 pins/week (mix of new and repins)

#### TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- **Format:** Short-form video (15–60 seconds). Room styling time-lapses, "print and frame with me," trend commentary, decor tips.
- **Tone:** Slightly more casual and personal than feed posts. Show the human behind the brand. Still clean and warm — not chaotic or overly edited.
- **Audio:** Trending sounds when they fit naturally. Soft, ambient music for styling videos. Voiceover for tips and commentary.
- **Text on screen:** Brief, readable, DM Sans or a clean sans-serif. Never more than two lines.
- **Posting cadence:** 2–3/week

### Email
- **Tone:** Warm and personal, like a note from a friend with good taste
- **Format:** Short, image-led. One clear message per email. Never more than one CTA.
- **Subject lines:** Lowercase, conversational. "new prints just dropped" not "🌿 NEW COLLECTION ALERT! 🌿"
- **Cadence:** 1–2/month max. New collections, seasonal roundups, occasional styling tips. Never spam.

### Blog
- **Tone:** Informative, warm, and visually rich
- **Topics:** Styling guides, trend recaps, botanical art history, "how to" content for digital downloads
- **Format:** 500–1,000 words with multiple images. Scannable with headers and short paragraphs.
- **SEO focus:** Target long-tail keywords like "vintage botanical gallery wall ideas," "how to print digital art at home," "affordable wall art for first apartment"
- **Cadence:** 2–4 posts/month
